About CaLi

CaLi is a lattice-based model to classify licenses. CaLi classifications are able to automatically answer questions like, what are the licenses that are compliant with a specific license? And what are the licenses that are compatible with a specific license? This license-based search engine is powered by a CaLi Classification and is able to classify licensed datasets.

The source code of this work is available on GitHub under the MIT license

Cali Search Engine is a service created by Benjamin MOREAU with GDD research group - LS2N, University of Nantes and OpenDataSoft company.
